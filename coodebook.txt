
The experiments have been carried out with a group of 30 volunteers(Subjects) within an age bracket of 19-48 years. Each person performed six activities (WALKING, WALKING_UPSTAIRS, WALKING_DOWNSTAIRS, SITTING, STANDING, LAYING) wearing a smartphone (Samsung Galaxy S II) on the waist, which are labaled as following:

 1. WALKING (label 1)
 2. WALKING_UPSTAIRS (label 2)
 3. WALKING_DOWNSTAIRS (label 3)
 4. SITTING (label 4)
 5. STANDING (label 5)
 6. LAYING (label 6)
